# Explore the DevPulse Sidebar

DevPulse lives in your Activity Bar. Click the pulse icon to reveal four views:

- **Today's Activity** \u2014 Live stats, work breakdown, language usage
- **Insights & Coaching** \u2014 AI-generated tips and quick stats
- **Projects & Languages** \u2014 All-time project breakdown
- **Focus & Flow** \u2014 Manage and review focus sessions

DevPulse tracks your activity automatically in the background \u2014 no manual timers needed.
