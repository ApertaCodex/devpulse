# Explore the DevPulse Sidebar

DevPulse lives in your Activity Bar. Click the pulse icon to reveal four views:

- **Today's Activity** — Live stats, work breakdown, language usage
- **Insights & Coaching** — AI-generated tips and quick stats
- **Projects & Languages** — All-time project breakdown
- **Focus & Flow** — Manage and review focus sessions

DevPulse tracks your activity automatically in the background — no manual timers needed.
