# AI-Powered Insights

DevPulse analyzes your coding patterns to generate:

- **Productivity Score** (0\u2013100) based on work intent and focus
- **Peak Hours** \u2014 when you're most productive
- **Context Switch Warnings** \u2014 when you're switching too much
- **Personalized Tips** \u2014 actionable coaching based on your data

All analysis happens locally \u2014 no data leaves your machine.
